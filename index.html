One of the first members, central to the leadership of the crew and one of the "triggers" of the One Piece world.

Nami

The first member of the crew. Extremely loyal and steadfast, he has few goals and sticks to them.

Roronoa Zoro

The third member of the crew, a soft character with lots of nuance and kindness.

One of the best antagonists, he exemplifies the evils of the World Government and is the driving force of many events.
